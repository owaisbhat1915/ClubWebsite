Club History

Football Pioneers

"Meet the Football Pioneers: In 2012, a group of trailblazers stepped onto the pitch, igniting a passion for the beautiful game that still burns bright today. These pioneers weren't just players they were architects of our footballing heritage. Through their skill, dedication, and love for the sport, they laid the groundwork for the victories and memories that define our club. Their legacy inspires us to continue their journey, honoring their contributions with every kick and every goal."

2012

Field Apprentices

In 2017, a new generation of football enthusiasts stepped onto the field as Field Apprentices. Learning from seasoned players, they honed their skills, embraced teamwork, and embodied the spirit of the game. Fuelled by passion and guided by the wisdom of their mentors, these apprentices left an indelible mark on the pitch, showcasing their potential as the future stars of football."

2017

Pitch Proteges

In 2023, the Pitch Proteges rose to prominence, guided by seasoned mentors and driven by their unwavering dedication to the game. Their relentless pursuit of excellence culminated in a remarkable achievement: clinching the A-Division championship title. With each match, they showcased their skill, unity, and determination, proving themselves as the future luminaries of football. As they lifted the championship trophy, they not only celebrated victory but also honored the legacy of their mentors. The Pitch Proteges of 2023 have etched their names in history, inspiring generations with their remarkable triumph."

2023
